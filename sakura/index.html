Sakura Super Mario Bros.

Sakura Super Mario Bros. DS is a complete overhaul of New Super Mario Bros. Featuring numerous new enemies, 80+ levels, never-before-seen themes, and a completely original soundtrack! The mod features an item shop system and native currency (Shuriken Coins) that you can collect in the levels. The mod also includes a completely original playable character, completely new world maps, and more!
